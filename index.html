How to Play

1. Enter Your Name

Type your name and submit your picks. One submission per person.

2. Pick Who Advances

For every match from the Round of 16 to the Final, simply pick which team goes through. Your picks cascade — the winners you choose move to the next round automatically.

3. Scoring

  • 3 PTS Correct Champion — Pick the team that wins the Final
  • 2 PTS Correct Finalist — Pick a team that reaches the Final
  • 1 PT Correct advancement — Each correct pick in R16, QF, or SF
  • 0 PTS Wrong pick — Team gets eliminated

4. Win!

Most points at the end takes the crown. Good luck!
